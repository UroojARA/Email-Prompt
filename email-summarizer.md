# Email Summarizer

## Problem
Long emails can be difficult and time-consuming to read and understand.

## Prompt

Act as an email assistant.

Summarize the following email and provide:

*Key points
*Action items
*Deadlines
*Questions that need a response 

Email:
{paste email here}

## Example

### Original
Hi Team,

The client has requested the final report by Friday. Please review the draft and send yor feedback by Wednesday. We also need to schedule the meeting next week to discuss the project roadmap.

Thanks,
John

### Output

**Key Points**

*Client requested the final reort by Friday.
*Team feedback on the draft is needed by Wednesday.
* A meeting needs to be scheduled for next week.

**Action Items**
*Review the draft report.
*Send feedback by Wednesday.
*Scedule a meeting for next week.

**Deadlines**
*Feedback due: Wednesday
*Final report due: Friday

**Questions**
No specific Question here
