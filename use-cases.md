# Use Cases

## Overview

The Email Prompt Toolkit is designed to improve professional email communication by transforming informal messages into polished emails and summarizing lengthly email converstaions. These prompts can be applied across a variety of workplace and professional communication scenarios.

---

## Professional Email Writer 

This prompt can be used to:

 - Rewrite informal messages into professional emails while preserving the original meaning
 - Request project feedback from a manager or team lead.
 - Reschedule meetings in a polite and professional manner.
 - Draft emails to colleagues, clients, or stakeholders.
 - Write interview follow-up and job application emails.
 - Improve the tone, grammer, and clarity of business communication.

---

## Email Summarizer

This prompt can be used to:

 - Summarize long email conversations.
 - Identify key discussion points
 - Extract action items and responsibilities.
 - Highlights deadlines and important dates.
 - Identify questions that require responses.
 - Save time by providing concise email summaries.

---

## Benefit

Using these prompts helps user:

 - Communicate more professionally.
 - Save time when writing or reading emails.
 - Maintain a consistent and respectful tone.
 - Focus on important information without reading lengthly email threads.
